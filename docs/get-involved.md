# Get Involved

Thank you for your interest in supporting Spokane Food Justice! There are many ways to get involved and make a difference in our community's food system.

## Step One

<v-icon icon="wpf--survey" start></v-icon>

If you live in the Chief Garry Park neighborhood in Spokane, please fill out [our survey](https://actionnetwork.org/surveys/cgp-neighborhood-interest?source=get_involved&) (even if you aren't sure if you are ready to be involved yet).

## Step Two

<v-icon icon="mdi--email-fast-outline" start></v-icon>

Watch for regular emails from us!  As we get this idea off the ground we will be planning get-togethers and other events to build community.

## Contact Us

For more information about getting involved, please reach out:

**Email:** [info@spokanefoodjustice.org](mailto:info@spokanefoodjustice.org)
