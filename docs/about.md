# About Spokane Food Sovereignty

## Who We Are

Spokane Food Sovereignty is a non-profit organization committed to creating a more just and sustainable food system in our community. We believe that everyone deserves access to healthy, affordable, and culturally appropriate food.

## Our Mission

To build community power and food sovereignty in Spokane by:

- Supporting local food production and distribution
- Educating the community about sustainable agriculture
- Advocating for food justice and equitable access
- Creating networks of support for local farmers and food producers
- Preserving traditional food knowledge and practices

## Our Values

### Community

We center community voices and needs in all our work, recognizing that those most impacted by food insecurity should lead solutions.

### Sustainability

We promote environmentally responsible practices that protect our land, water, and air for future generations.

### Equity

We work to dismantle systemic barriers to food access and ensure all community members can thrive.

### Respect

We honor diverse food traditions, cultures, and knowledge systems within our community.
